Perhaps you've heard this important lesson
from the philosopher Maimonides before.
The main message here is that you can do
something for someone and it will be of help
But if you don't teach them how to do it themselves,
they will forever be independent. That is one of the
most beautiful things about teaching and education.
Once you share or obtain knowledge there is power
and capability obtained forever.
